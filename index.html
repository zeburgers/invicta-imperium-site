How to Play

Install a MUD client, then connect using the host and port above. Create a character in-game and step into the world.

In-game, start with help. If you hit a wall, the Discord is there to get you unstuck.

From the Founder

I've been playing MUDs for the better part of 25 years. In that time, I've seen many systems and many things that I feel would be good in a MUD and have always wanted to build a MUD that is a living, breathing world you can play in alone or with others. Invicta Imperium is the culmination of my time in the MUD world. I hope to garner a community that will help me grow my vision from something that I've only dreamed of into a reality.

I want to create a world that allows players near-complete agency. GM-run events are going to happen to help the players' story along, not the GM's story.

  • You want to be a lanista? Buy a ludus.
  • You want to be a gladiator? Join a ludus.
  • You want to be a legionary? Join the legion.
  • Shop owner? Buy a shop, build your stock, start selling.

With enough players, I can put players into nearly every role, with systems to enhance and back up their roleplay, not replace and definitely (hopefully) not to limit it.

So please, join me in creating this world and hopefully you find yourself a new home to MUD in, grab some friends, new or old, and try to ascend the social ranks. Who knows, maybe you'll be a senator come the next Civic Interlude?

Roadmap

Live major systems: Combat, basic crafting, medicine/wounds, calendar/time.

Next: Map expansion (more districts, places to live/work/gather, and room for society to breathe).

After: Society structure (status/roles/factions), expanded crafting, expanded combat, expanded medicine/wounds.

Direction, not deadlines. Systems evolve through play.
